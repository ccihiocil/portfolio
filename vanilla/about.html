Greeting from MierCat

I am Mier Chen, a data scientist and photographer, focusing on research and creating imagery that traced the value and beauty of natural wonders. I recorded the damage caused by human disregard. The ethereal landscape and the playful wildlife captured in my photographs aim to remind us of the pure air, soil, water, and land that we are losing.

May my photographs brings you back to the ideals of Eden.

My Love towards the Nature

I fell in love with photography as I travel across the nation aiming to see myself the real America. In these six years of overseas studying, I have lived in Washington, Virginia, and California, and visited over 40 states.

Photography Style

Future

In the future, I would love to apply more of what I learn as a data scientist and statistician. Using the numbers to strengthen the power of the images. I want to work with the researchers, environmental scientists and the editors to collect the data, pictures, and stories on the issues related to nature, wildlife and ecological issues.
